The Setting

Dublin Ireland, 1904

"Ulysses" by James Joyce is a modernist masterpiece set in Dublin, following Leopold Bloom's meandering journey through a single day, paralleling Homer's Odyssey. Joyce intricately weaves stream-of-consciousness narratives, experimental language, and diverse literary styles, exploring themes of identity, mortality, and the human condition.
